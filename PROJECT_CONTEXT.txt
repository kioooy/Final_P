# 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

## Project Name

TechZone AI

## Business Domain

Technology Devices E-Commerce Platform

TechZone AI is a mobile application for selling technology products and electronic devices online.

Customers can browse products, compare specifications, purchase items, locate physical stores using Google Maps, and receive intelligent recommendations from a Gemini AI Shopping Assistant.
